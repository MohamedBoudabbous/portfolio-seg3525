eHotels Management System

Hotel Management System basé sur la modélisation relationnelle, PostgreSQL, Spring Boot et des règles métier liées aux réservations.

PostgreSQL Spring Boot SQL Database Design

Project Overview

eHotels is a complete hotel management system designed to manage hotel chains, hotels, rooms, employees, clients, reservations and rentals.

The project focuses on database modeling, business logic, relational schemas and reservation management in a realistic context.

Database Modeling

ER Diagram

The project includes a complete Entity-Relationship diagram representing hotel chains, hotels, rooms, clients, reservations and rentals.

Mermaid Representation

A technical Mermaid representation was also created to support the transition toward implementation.

Relational Schema and Constraints

Relational Schema

The relational model defines primary keys, foreign keys, normalization and main constraints.

Constraints and Business Rules

Advanced constraints structure date consistency, room availability and history preservation.

Technical Highlights

Relational Modeling

Reservation Logic

Modeling of reservation-to-rental conversion and room availability.
